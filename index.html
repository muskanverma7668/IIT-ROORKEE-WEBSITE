About Us

SMJN (PG) College, Haridwar is one of the oldest and most reputed educational institutes of Uttarakhand. Established in 1960, the college is affiliated to Shri Dev Suman Uttarakhand State University and offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams.

Our campus features modern labs, a rich library, sports facilities, and a vibrant student community. We are committed to academic excellence and holistic development, empowering students for a bright future.
